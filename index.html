    Project Ideas

    Overview:

    The project I am pursuing is an all-device application that allows people in Engineering Universities, Jobs and Research & Development centers to quickly solve all sorts of engineering related questions by using the App that will have a library of every equation used with the ability to visualize their obtained results through graphs and related figures as an option to better understand the output. This app will have a simplified app surface with the option of selecting an advanced version in order to input their own raw values in its entirety if they wished to.

    Motivation:

    From a general persons view this app may seem boring, but for millions of engineers from all disciplines and experience levels out their this will be a timesaver as it cuts down the time wasted on typing the equations onto the calculator with the entire equation format which could have been better utilized for other works. I am a university student who has a low-tech calculator that doesn’t have the latest capabilities of pre-set equations where I only have to input my values, which takes completing my math intensive assignments 2 to 3 times longer than others with better calculators.

    Description:

    The app will be available on desktop with full support for Android and Apple Operating systems across all devices and it will also have all of the mathematical equations (millions of them) stored in a cloud server which can be accessed by thousands at a time, The App interface for each equation will be simplified with an option at the bottom that allows an “Advanced Mode” to be activated where the preset values of certain figures that are considered a ‘constant’ can be changed to own desired values with additional abilities to control the decimal/Significant figures output for results as well an in-built scanner that can identify the name of a handwritten equation if the user were to not remember the name of the equation type.

    The app will allow those who are new to engineering to also have an understanding of what they calculated by using the graphs and figures that come below the results output section which will also show how the answer was obtained step-by-step that would also help those who are at an academic level to understand how the equation results are obtained and how to understand them.

    The app will have a built-In scanner that will scan equations off most surfaces with app updates that would make the function as well as the entire app more user-friendly to use as the months go by. we expect crashes to occur at the initial stages right after app launch on all app stores as well as glitches and issues with user access of the cloud server if and when the number of users increases. We also expect to get issues with the scanning feature which might vary results depending on the camera quality of the phone used as well as the surface off which it is trying to scan the equation from, but we expect to give monthly updates for all features of our app to make sure that the inconvenience faced by users are at a minimum.The app interface will be simple where as you enter the app a search bar at the top will be seen which you can manually search equations for and a camera button next to it which will be the scan function and in the middle will be a quick use list that shows all the equations the user has used in recent times and if it’s your first time using the app then a guide on how to use the app and functions that are available with prompt you to follow instructions on what the app can do and how to navigate around the app. It will also have a report button right below the middle section that will allow people to send crash and glitch data on the app when it detects a crash in its system, the data will be sent to our analysis team that will find root cause and follow up with updates by the end of the month. The app will also support advertisements for revenue.
